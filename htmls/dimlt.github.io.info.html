1.5   live2dw(网页小猫使用步骤)

No. 描述
First 拷贝这整个文件夹(live2dw)
Second html导入该JS文件: live2dw/lib/L2Dwidget.js
Thirdly html写网页小猫初始化的JS方法L2Dwidget.init({"pluginRootPath":"live2dw/","pluginJsPath":"lib/","pluginModelPath":"assets/","tagMode":false,"log":false,"model":{"jsonPath":"live2dw/assets/hijiki.model.json"},"display":{"position":"right","width":150,"height":300},"mobile":{"show":true}});
